WERKSTATT 8

Werkstatt 8 is a zine showcasing latest product and material design from Hochschule für Gestaltung in Offenbach. Their functions, interactions and features, as well as use scenarios are shown and explained through line art graphics.

The pubblication includes theoretical articles and speculative use cases. The are also some aestetics inserts which try to represent the interactions with materials in a more metaphorical way.

In the end there is also a Spotify playlist curated by the editors. The mood it tires to convey is a mesh of post-punk, with his technological and social themes, and psychedelia, with his mellow mood.
